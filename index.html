Teaching Philosophy

I believe teaching is a journey intertwined with lifelong learning. As a teacher and a learner, I continuously update my knowledge and skills to align with the ever-changing educational landscape. My teaching philosophy is built on five foundational pillars, each contributing to the holistic development of students in an interconnected world.

Lifelong Education

Education transcends formal schooling, encompassing a continuous pursuit of knowledge and personal growth. Inspired by the concept of a growth mindset (Dweck, 2006), I instill in my students the perseverance and resilience to view challenges as opportunities for growth. By fostering this mindset, I prepare them to navigate an evolving society with confidence and adaptability.

Collaborative Learning

Learning is most effective when it is a collective endeavor. I create a collaborative classroom environment where students are empowered to share ideas, challenge each other, and learn from diverse perspectives. Group activities, discussions, and peer assessments help build communication skills and a strong sense of community, enhancing their overall educational journey.

Balanced Assessments

Assessment is a vital tool for understanding and improvement. I employ both formative and summative assessments to support learning

Formative assessments

provide ongoing feedback, enabling me to adapt instruction to individual needs.

Summative assessments

measure cumulative understanding and guide students toward achieving their educational milestones, such as G.C.E. Ordinary Level and Advanced Level qualifications.

This balanced approach encourages students to actively participate in their learning process.

Technology in ELT

Modern education demands the integration of technology to enhance learning. Utilizing the TPACK framework (Harris, Mishra & Koehler, 2009), I balance content knowledge, pedagogy, and technological tools to create engaging lessons. By incorporating multimedia, AI, and online resources, I ensure lessons are efficient and effective. Additionally, I emphasize digital citizenship, teaching students about online safety, information literacy, and ethical behavior in the digital realm.

Extracurricular Activities

Extracurricular activities are essential for holistic development. For students, they foster teamwork, leadership, and mindfulness while providing social interaction and stress relief. For teachers, they build stronger student relationships and enhance professional satisfaction. These activities complement classroom learning, creating a well-rounded educational experience.

Education is a lifelong journey enriched by collaboration, technology, diverse assessments, and extracurricular engagement. By fostering a growth mindset and a dynamic learning environment, I empower my students to embrace challenges and unlock their full potential for a brilliant future.
